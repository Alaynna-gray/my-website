What the different zodiacs mean about you

  • Aries

    Information:

    You are shy, energetic, naive at times, and you let things get to your head. You
    don’t like school unless you’re up and moving around. You have a heart of gold
    and that can be a weakness and strength , for you could be taken advantage of.

  • Taurus

    Information:

    You are shy most of the time, stubborn, and hard too please. You pick friends carefully
    and it’s usually quality over quantity. Loyal to loved ones and friends. You don’t get
    hurt very often.

  • Gemini

    Information:

    You are outgoing most of the time, trusting, jealous, cocky at times and you don’t like the
    outdoors and rather spend your time inside. You are a very trusting person. sometimes you can
    come off as rude. You don’t let anyone tell you help you because you like figuring things out
    for yourself.

  • Cancer

    Information:

    You can be loving, unpredictable at times, overly sensitive with certain things, difficult,
    empathetic, and outgoing. Can be both lazy and active, it depends on the mood. You are very
    caring and sees the good in everybody which can also be a weakness.

  • Leo

    Information:

    You are passionate, love attention and spotlight, jealous, and respectful. You value respect
    over anything and will tell let someone know if they'er showing disrespect. You also have
    many friends and considered popular.

  • Virgo

    Information:

    You are nurturing, mature, shy, sensitive, and non-judgmental. You are very controlled and
    no when enough is enough. You let people win arguments in fear of starting something or
    losing a friend.

  • Libra

    Information:

    You are a perfectionist, hardworking in everything, and is quiet but not necessarily
    shy. You want everything perfect and gets frustrated when it’s not. You can work with
    others efficiently but don’t like doing so.

  • Scorpio

    Information:

    You are mentally strong, shows no emotion, can be inconsiderate, is mostly outgoing and
    you are loyal but sometimes say insensitive things and do not realize what they say till after
    it's said.

  • Sagittarius

    Information:

    You are a leader, angered easily, helpful, and kind hearted. Although they are easily angered,
    they still are helpful and work with others. They purposefully surround themselves with
    people who are less successful so they feel better about their fortunes.

  • Capricorn

    Information:

    You are kind, welcoming, outgoing, and a leader. You help anyone through anything and
    always put them above you in every circumstance. You are very caring but have a hard time
    listening and too busy giving advice.

  • Aquarius

    Information:

    You are shy and outgoing, annoyed easily, controlled, and loyal. You will never allow anyone
    to disrespect or tell you to do something they don't agree with. It depends on the type of people
    you're with, sometimes shy and sometimes not.

  • Pisces

    Information:

    You are helpful, wise, artistic, and stubborn. You will argue till you win and you will always
    win and when you don't they get mad or upset. You love art and you're very passionate about it.
